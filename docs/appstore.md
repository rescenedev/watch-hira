## English (en-US)

**Name**
```
KanaStudy
```

**Subtitle**
```
Hiragana, Katakana & JLPT
```

**Promotional Text**
```
One character at a glance. From the 46 hiragana to JLPT vocab and travel phrases — learn Japanese on your wrist, with no ads and no account. Swipe to graduate words and get a gentle morning review reminder.
```

**Description**
```
Japanese, the light way.

KanaStudy helps you learn Japanese in short, frequent bites on iPhone and Apple Watch — from hiragana and katakana to JLPT vocabulary and travel phrases. No account, no ads, no tracking. Open it and you’re learning.

■ Start with the characters
· Hiragana & katakana flashcards (46 basic + dakuten/handakuten/yoon)
· Shuffled every time you enter
· Tap to reveal romaji and example words

■ Multiple-choice quiz
· 10 questions: see a character, pick the reading
· Best score saved per script
· Missed items come back until you get them twice

■ JLPT & travel vocabulary
· JLPT N3, N4, N5 core words and travel phrases
· Kanji, furigana and meaning together
· A short everyday example sentence per word

■ Learned words & spaced review
· Words you flip pile up by day (Today, Yesterday, …)
· A morning reminder brings one back — tap to jump straight to review
· Swipe a word you know: 2 days → 1 week → 1 month, then it graduates to the Archive

■ Hear it
· Tap to hear pronunciation (auto-detects Japanese / English)

■ Apple Watch
· Study, quiz and review right on your wrist
· A complication shows a fresh kana every hour

■ Your own deck (iOS)
· Import your existing Anki .apkg decks

Works fully offline; your progress stays on your device.

— Some example sentences come from the Tatoeba Project (CC BY).
```

```

```
First release. Hiragana & katakana, JLPT & travel vocab, quizzes, learned-words by date with spaced review, and Apple Watch. Start light, on your wrist.

## App Privacy (개인정보 보호)
- **Data Not Collected** — 수집하는 데이터 없음. (네트워크 미사용, 로컬 저장만)
- 별도 데이터 타입 선택 없이 “Data is not collected from this app” 체크.

## Export Compliance (수출 규정)
- 표준 암호화 외 사용 없음 → **암호화 사용 안 함(Exempt)**.
- `ITSAppUsesNonExemptEncryption = NO` (Info.plist에 추가 권장).
